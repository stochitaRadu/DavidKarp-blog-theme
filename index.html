Hello,I am David.

I am a web developer,widely known as the creator of Tumblr.I like cats.

I began my career as an intern under Fred Seibert at the animation company Frederator Studios, where I built the studio's first blogging platform and conceived, wrote, and edited their first internet video network, Channel Frederator. Pretty cool,huh?

I went on to work for online parenting forum UrbanBaby until it was sold to CNET in 2006. Then I started my own software consulting company, Davidville, where I worked with computer engineer Marco Arment on projects for clients.

During a gap between contracts in 2006, the two began work on a microblogging website, which was launched as Tumblr in February 2007.
